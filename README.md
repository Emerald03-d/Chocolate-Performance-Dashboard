# Chocolate Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes chocolate sales performance across multiple dimensions including time, product, region, and salespersons. The goal is to provide actionable insights into revenue trends, top-performing products, and overall sales performance.

## 🚀 Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends over time
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional sales performance
- Evaluate salesperson contributions

## 📌 Dashboard Features
- Executive KPI Overview (Total Revenue, Quantity Sold, Orders, Average Order Value)
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Revenue by Year
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Quantity Sold by Product
- Revenue Contribution by Product
- Revenue by Country
- Quantity Sold by Country
- Revenue by Salesperson (Top performers highlighted)
- Interactive filters (Date, Product, Country, Salesperson)

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling

## 📈 Key Insights
- The business generated over $19M in total revenue.
- A few products contribute significantly to total revenue.
- Australia and the UK are top-performing regions.
- Sales performance varies across salespersons.
- Revenue trends indicate steady growth over time.
